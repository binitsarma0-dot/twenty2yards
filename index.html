Training Pathways

OUR PROGRAMS

Structured training for every level — from first-time players to competitive cricketers aiming for district and state selection.

🏏
Junior Academy

Foundational cricket training designed for young players. Focus on basics, discipline, and love of the game.

  • Age group: 6–14 years
  • Batting, bowling & fielding basics
  • Fun match practice included
  • Certified coaches on duty
🎯
Senior Competitive

Advanced training for players targeting district, state, or selection-level cricket. Intensive and performance-focused.

  • Age group: 15–25 years
  • Video analysis & technique refinement
  • Regular competitive matches
  • Tournament & league exposure
👩
Girls Cricket

One of Delhi's finest dedicated girls cricket programs. Safe, encouraging, and professional environment.

  • All age groups welcome
  • Female-friendly atmosphere
  • Regular girls' matches & tournaments
  • Pathway to U-19 & national selection
🏟️
Match Program

Regular intra-academy and inter-academy matches with real pressure situations to build match temperament.

  • Weekly practice matches
  • League matches & tournaments
  • Cricket tours organized
  • 60-metre boundary ground
💪
Personal Training

One-on-one sessions with expert coaches for accelerated improvement in specific skills.

  • Customized training plan
  • Individual attention
  • Gym & fitness support
  • Flexible scheduling
🏠
Residential Program

For outstation players, a full boarding + cricket program so you can train without distractions.

  • Hostel facility available
  • Full-time training schedule
  • Open to all-India students
  • Meals & accommodation
